ABOUT SAN FRANCISCO

San Francisco, in Northern California, is a hilly city at the tip of a peninsula surrounded by the Pacific Ocean and San Francisco Bay. It is famous for its permanent fog, the iconic Golden Gate Bridge, its Cable Cars and colorful Victorian houses. The Transamerica Pyramid, located in the financial district, is the most distinctive skyscraper in the city. In the bay is the island of Alcatraz, which houses the old prison.

MY EXPERIENCE IN SAN FRANCISCO

My experience in San Francisco has been very rewarding. I was attracted by this start-up / new technologies aspect, working methods different from those practiced in France and an ease in doing business if you are motivated. In San Francisco, I was surprised by the very open minds of people. Thanks to this experience, I learned a lot about myself, to manage on my own in an unknown environment. I now have more self-confidence, I was able to open up more to people, who was very closed in on myself. I was also able to improve my English.
